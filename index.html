A Legacy of Light

Founded on the principles of artistry and precision, Aethelred & Co. has been a purveyor of fine jewelry and horology since 1888. Our creations are not mere accessories; they are whispered stories of heritage, designed to be cherished for generations.

Each gemstone is ethically sourced and hand-selected, each timepiece assembled by master artisans. We blend timeless aesthetics with innovative techniques to create pieces that are both classic and contemporary.

The Journey of a Masterpiece

From conception to creation, every step is a testament to our dedication.

01. The Vision

Our journey begins with an inspiration—a fleeting moment, a historical motif, or the raw beauty of a gemstone. This vision is translated into initial sketches, capturing the soul of the future piece.

02. Material Selection

We traverse the globe to source the world's most exceptional materials. Only the finest conflict-free diamonds, lustrous pearls, and precious metals meet our exacting standards.

03. Master Craftsmanship

In our atelier, master jewelers and watchmakers dedicate hundreds of hours to bring the design to life. Their hands shape, set, and polish each component with unwavering precision and passion.

04. The Final Polish

The final piece undergoes rigorous quality control, ensuring it is flawless. It is then placed in its signature case, ready to begin its journey with you—a timeless emblem of luxury.
